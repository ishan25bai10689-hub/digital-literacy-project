In this task, I learned how to write professional emails and follow proper email etiquette. I wrote two emails: one to request an assignment extension and another to apply for an internship opportunity.

I also created a checklist of do’s and don’ts for social media use. This helped me understand how to behave responsibly online.

A situation where poor communication can cause problems is when someone sends an email without a subject or uses informal language. It can create a bad impression. This can be avoided by writing clear, polite, and properly structured emails.

This task improved my communication skills and made me more confident in writing formal emails.
